Slide 64. The Power of Community, but with the y crossed out and rewritten as Communities. The multicolor Boba-tans are back again, and form a multitude of connections; in some cases they have formed small groups by finding each other with the similar interests and shared experiences (same color jackets), and in other groups, the jacket colors are diversified. Each community is interlinked with the larger overall fandom community by their relationships with one another.
