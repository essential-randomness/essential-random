2020-present: cancellation Google canceled its 2020 April Fools' jokes for the
first time due to the COVID-19 pandemic, urging employees to contribute to
relief efforts instead.

Since the cancellation in 2020, Google has not participated in April Fools.
However in 2020, April 1st was celebrated with the anniversary of Jean
Macnamara's birthday.
